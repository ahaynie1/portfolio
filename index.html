About

Allison Haynie is a multimedia artist with a background in visual and performing arts that strongly influences her worldview and work. She also has years of business and marketing experience that includes project managing and art directing a charity fashion show, placing internationally in DECA Sports and Entertainment Marketing competitions, and serving on the Junior Advisory Board for the Spokane County Interstate Fair. She is currently studying visual communication design, user experience design, and theatre at Eastern Washington University. Alli has a passion for watching women’s sports and highlighting the underappreciated talent in that industry through doodles and tweets.
